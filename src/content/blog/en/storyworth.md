Choosing the right platform to preserve your memories is a meaningful decision. Whether you're writing your autobiography, documenting a childhood, sharing a travel adventure, or capturing the legacy of your family, you want a solution that's personal, flexible, and easy to use.

Both **Storyworth** and [**Life Story AI**](https://life-story.ai/) offer tools to help you capture and preserve life stories. But **[Life Story AI](https://life-story.ai/) takes it several steps further**, with cutting-edge AI features, advanced collaboration options, and unmatched global accessibility.

Here's how Life Story AI compares to Storyworth in 2025:

---

### Smart AI Assistance, Personalized and Powerful

**Storyworth** sends fixed weekly questions chosen from a limited library.

[**Life Story AI**](https://life-story.ai/) uses artificial intelligence to **personalize questions each week**, adapting to your story, your tone, and your past answers. Whether you're reflecting on a childhood memory or a transformative life event, our AI grows with your narrative.

Plus, Life Story AI doesn't just ask questions. It **helps you write**. Our AI assistant improves your grammar, enriches your writing style, and helps you express yourself with clarity and emotion.

---

### Multilingual Voice-to-Text, Tell Your Story Your Way

Storyworth only works in English and relies entirely on typing.

Life Story AI includes **advanced multilingual voice-to-text** that works in over **100 languages and accents**, even in noisy environments. Prefer speaking to writing? Just talk, and we'll transcribe and organize everything beautifully for you.

---

### Write Any Kind of Story, Not Just a Memoir

While Storyworth focuses on traditional memoir questions, Life Story AI offers **total creative freedom**. You can write:

- An autobiography
- A birth story
- A travel diary
- A tribute to a loved one
- A collaborative family history
- And so much more

Our AI adapts to any format or tone: casual, poetic, reflective, or humorous, so your book truly sounds like you.

---

### Collaborative Writing, Made Simple

Storyworth books are limited to one writer per account.

With Life Story AI, you can **collaborate in real time** with family members, friends, or co-authors. Whether you're capturing your parents' memories or writing a book as a couple, Life Story AI offers **advanced collaborative modes** with shared timelines, feedback options, and contribution tracking.

---

### Modern Book Covers and Global Delivery

While Storyworth books have a standard design, Life Story AI offers a selection of **modern, premium-quality book covers** to match your story's tone and style. You can also customize your title, back cover text, and dedication.

And yes, we **ship worldwide**.

---

### Affordable Extra Copies, $25 vs. up to $99

Here's one of the biggest differences: **the price of extra printed copies**.

- **Storyworth** charges **between $39 and $99** for each additional hardcover book.
- **Life Story AI** offers **beautiful printed books for just $25 each**, making it easy and affordable to share with the whole family.

Plus, we've made it easy for your loved ones to **order their own copies directly**, with no bulk orders and no hassle.
