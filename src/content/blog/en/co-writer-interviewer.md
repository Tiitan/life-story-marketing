# Write Your Story... Together!

At Life Story AI, we believe the strongest stories are rarely written alone. They grow from shared memories, thoughtful questions, and the point of view of the people who know us best.

That is why we are excited to introduce a new collaboration feature.

## Two Ways to Invite Loved Ones Into the Journey

You can now invite family members, friends, or loved ones to contribute to your story in two different ways.

### As a Co-Writer

A co-writer helps you shape and improve your story.

They receive the same weekly email questions as you, can reply, review, or add to your stories, and have full access to the book.

This is ideal for:

- Co-authoring a family story
- Sharing memories across generations
- Moving faster with help from a motivated loved one

### As an Interviewer

An interviewer does not have access to the book, but can ask you questions whenever inspiration strikes.

Those questions can be fun, reflective, spontaneous, or surprising, such as:

> What was your favorite movie as a kid?

> Why did you decide to move to Japan instead of somewhere else?

You can choose to answer or skip a question. If you do answer, it becomes a new story in your book.

It is a simple way to surface new memories and make storytelling feel more connected.

---

Until now, Lisa, our AI assistant, was the one asking personalized questions each week to help you write. Now your loved ones can join in too, bringing their own curiosity, memories, and perspective.

Your life story becomes a dialogue, not just a monologue.
