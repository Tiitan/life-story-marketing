Hello,

We have some excellent news to share with you.

Based on preliminary interviews conducted by real biographers, we've developed a new personalized interview experience.

This major enhancement will help Lisa, our AI, guide you through many more kinds of nonfiction stories.
For example: the story of immigrating to a new country, the story of a family member told by someone else, the story of a romantic encounter, the story of your professional life, the story of a memorable trip, or the little and big stories of everyday life.

There are no limits to the type of story you can tell. You are completely free.

## How does it work?

Lisa will ask you targeted questions about your writing project, such as:

- Who will be the main author of your story?
- What is the theme of your story: your family, yourself, a birth, a journey, an encounter, a loved one, etc.?
- Who is your story for, and why are you writing it?

Or any other subject that matters to you. It is entirely up to you. Lisa adapts to your answers, then asks follow-up questions to better understand your project.

Based on your answers, Lisa will generate six new customized questions to get an even clearer picture of what you want to create.

In this way, she can offer more personalized chaptering, and her weekly questions become even more relevant and better adapted to your story.

### Tell the story of your life, your family, or a loved one in a book

Let Lisa, your personal biographer, guide you.
