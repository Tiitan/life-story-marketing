This July brings some of our most exciting updates yet. We are thrilled to introduce two major features that make it easier than ever to create, share, and preserve your life stories with Life Story AI.

## Direct Ordering: Share Your Story Like Never Before

We have completely rethought how your loved ones can get copies of your life story book, and the result is wonderfully simple.

### No More Bulk Ordering Headaches

Gone are the days of guessing how many copies to order for family and friends. Our new Direct Ordering feature puts the process directly in their hands.

Here is how it works:

- Personalized sharing links let you generate unique ordering links from your book dashboard and share them with anyone who would like a copy.
- Seamless direct ordering means family and friends can order one or multiple copies and have them shipped straight to their door.
- Real-time notifications keep you informed about who has ordered copies through your dashboard.
- Smart QR code integration lets you print a QR code at the end of your book so readers can order their own copy or gift one to someone else.

### Why This Changes Everything

No more coordinating with a long list of relatives. No more worrying about storage after a bulk order. No more wondering if Aunt Mary really wanted that third copy. Your loved ones can order exactly what they need, when they need it, and you do not have to manage the logistics.

---

## Next-Generation Speech-to-Text: Breaking Down Language Barriers

Our speech-to-text engine has received a major upgrade, and we are incredibly proud of what it can now do.

### Crystal Clear Understanding

Life Story AI now understands speech with unprecedented accuracy. Whether you speak quickly, have an accent, or occasionally stumble over a word, the improved engine captures your meaning with remarkable precision.

### Global Language Support

We have expanded support from English and French to 10 major languages:

- English
- Spanish
- French
- German
- Hindi
- Russian
- Portuguese
- Japanese
- Italian
- Dutch

### Real-Time Multilingual Transcription

The most exciting part is that you can now speak in multiple languages during the same session.

Imagine telling a story about your grandmother's Italian recipes while naturally switching between English and Italian, or describing your business travels while blending English, Spanish, and German. Life Story AI recognizes the language switches in real time and transcribes everything accurately.

This breakthrough is especially helpful for:

- Multicultural families preserving stories in their heritage languages
- Multilingual storytellers who think and remember in more than one language
- Anyone who wants to include authentic foreign phrases or expressions

## What This Means for Your Life Story

These updates are more than just new features. They are tools that honor the authentic, multicultural, and interconnected way modern life stories are told.

Your memories do not live in one language or get shared in only one format. Now, neither does Life Story AI.
