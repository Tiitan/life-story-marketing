We are excited to share the latest updates to [Life Story AI](https://life-story.ai/), all designed to make your writing experience smoother, more personal, and easier to organize.

### Chapter Creation

You can now structure your books with chapters that group related stories together. Chapters are fully flexible, so you can rename them or move them around whenever you like. We also generate a table of contents automatically, making the final book easier to browse and read.

### Add Titles to Individual Stories

Stories can now include their own custom title. That title appears at the start of the story in the exported book, which makes each section easier to recognize and keeps the whole project better organized.

### Choose Lisa's Rewriting Style

Lisa can now rewrite your text in different styles, depending on the tone you want. The normal mode still improves your writing and corrects mistakes, but you can also choose romantic, intimate, poetic, humorous, or third-person rewriting.

### Add Captions to Photo Memories

Photo memories can now include captions. When your book is generated, the caption appears beneath the image so you can add context, a date, or a more personal note.

These updates give you more control over both the structure and the style of your book, while still keeping the writing process simple.
